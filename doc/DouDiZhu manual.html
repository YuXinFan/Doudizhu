Doudizhu

Three players doudizhu program with one suit card

Development Environment

Matlab 2019a
Matlab App designer for 2019a

How to start

Start a Client

  1. Enter the folder in matlab. Add enums and img folders to matlab path.
  2. Double click start.mlapp or enter start.mlapp in matlab comment line. A window appears as follows，
  3. Open a user client, push the button Open Client in the window.
  4. Open two more clients as step 3.

Enter a Room

  1. After openning a client, a client window appears. Type your username in type window and click start. You enter a room, a new room GUI appears.

Ready/Unready when waitting in room

  1. Click the ready button to change your ready status. Click no ready to ready or click ready to unready. 'Wait for 3 players enter the room and all players ready. Game start.

Play

  1. First you get 17 cards and two botton, one bid landlord button and another not bit landlord button. Click one of them.

  2. The landlord gets three more cards. And he play cards first.

  3. Play as normal Doudizhu with rule notify. game.

  4. When one player wins, a result status 'You lose' or "You Win!" shows. Then click the button 'Game Over' to go into room Waitting status.

Remark

Only support 1 room now. That is to say only support 3 players to play now.
